About

iWeb-Studio is a web design studio located in Tangerang, Indonesia founded by Heru Susanto. Specializing in the design and development of affordable custom websites for small and medium size businesses.
The owner and principal web designer, Heru Susanto aka “strato” has over 6 years of experience designing web sites for numerous business.

iWeb-Studio can provide a wide range of services including brand identity design, web design, web development, custom wordpress work, template design, niche website, and SEO.

Whether you are launching a brand new business online, updating the look of an existing web site or just need to establish an internet presence, iWeb-Studio will help make your internet venture a success.

Our services

Our main goal is to help small to medium-sized businesses build a strong online presence. We offer a variety of services to suit your needs, whether you need all the works (logo, domain, template design, website for business, ecommerce, niche website, SEO, social media services.. etc) or just occasional maintenance services, we are here to help.

Our clients are vary from start-ups, entrepreneurs , work at home people till established corporate that looking to expand their business to the web. We strive to provide superior design and service, delivering designs that create lasting impressions. We provide our clients with a web site that is not only attractive and memorable but also functional, affordable and easy to maintain.

As a small business, we understand the challenges that you face to set up your online presence. We work extensively to help you overcome those challenges and meet your goals.
